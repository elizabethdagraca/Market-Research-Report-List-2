<p><ul><li>Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters</li><li>Carbon Block Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fridge Filters Market Types - Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters and Carbon Block Filters are both popular choices for filtering water in refrigerators. GAC filters use natural carbon to capture impurities and improve taste and odor, whereas carbon block filters contain densely compacted carbon to provide more thorough filtration. GAC filters are typically more affordable but may need frequent replacements, while carbon block filters offer better contaminant removal but tend to come at a higher cost. Both options offer effective water filtration for refrigerators.</p></p>
