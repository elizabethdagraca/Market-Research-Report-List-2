<p><p>Polypropylene Cast Film market can be divided into four types based on thickness: Up to 30 Microns, 30-50 Microns, 51-70 Microns, and Above 70 Microns. Films up to 30 microns are commonly used for packaging applications like food wrapping. 30-50 micron films are used for applications requiring a bit more strength and durability. Films in the 51-70 micron range are typically used for heavy-duty packaging or industrial purposes. Films above 70 microns are used for applications requiring high strength and puncture resistance, such as construction and agriculture.</p></p>
