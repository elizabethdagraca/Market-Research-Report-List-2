<p><strong>The Sagittal Suture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable sutures</li><li>Non-absorbable sutures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sagittal suture market types include absorbable and non-absorbable sutures. Absorbable sutures are made from materials that break down in the body over time, eliminating the need for removal. They are ideal for superficial wounds and can be used in surgery. Non-absorbable sutures, on the other hand, are made from materials like nylon or polyester that do not break down in the body and require removal. They are preferred for deep wounds and areas of high tension where long-term support is needed.</p></p>
