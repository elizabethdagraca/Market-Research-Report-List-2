Waterbased marking paint is environmentally friendly and easy to apply. Solvent-based marking paint is long-lasting and resistant to wear and tear. Two-component road marking paint consists of a resin and a curing agent which reacts to form a durable coating. Each type of road marking paint offers different benefits and is used depending on specific requirements.</p></p>
