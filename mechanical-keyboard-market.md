<p><p>The mechanical keyboard market can be categorized into three main types based on the switches used:  </p><p>1. Linear switches offer a smooth keystroke without tactile feedback or audible click, making them popular for gaming and fast typing.  </p><p>2. Tactile non-clicky switches provide a subtle bump when the key is actuated, giving users feedback without the noise of a click, ideal for those who prefer a more tactile typing experience.  </p><p>3. Clicky switches have both a tactile bump and audible click when pressed, favored by users who appreciate the satisfying sound and feel of typing.</p></p>
