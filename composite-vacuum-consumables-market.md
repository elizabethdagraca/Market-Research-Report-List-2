<p><p>The Composite Vacuum Consumables market consists of various types of consumables used in the vacuum bagging process for composite material manufacturing. These include Vacuum Bagging Film, Release Film, Peel Ply, Breather & Bleeders, and other miscellaneous consumables. Vacuum Bagging Film is used to create a vacuum-tight seal around the composite material. Release Film prevents the resin from sticking to the vacuum bag. Peel Ply provides a textured surface for bonding. Breather & Bleeders allow for the removal of excess resin and air during the curing process. Others include additional materials such as sealant tape and vacuum connectors.</p></p>
