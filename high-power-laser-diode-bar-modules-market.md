<p><p>High Power Laser Diode Bar Modules come in two main types: Pulsed Laser Diode Bar and CW (Continuous Wave) Laser Diode Bar. </p><p>Pulsed Laser Diode Bars are used for applications that require high peak power output in short bursts, such as industrial cutting and welding. On the other hand, CW Laser Diode Bars provide a continuous output of laser power, making them suitable for applications like pumping solid-state lasers and material processing. Both types of modules play a crucial role in various industries, offering high power output for different applications.</p></p>
