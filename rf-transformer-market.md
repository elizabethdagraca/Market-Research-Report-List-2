<p><ul><li>Magnetic Coupling Transformer</li><li>Transmission Line Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF transformers can be categorized into two main types: magnetic coupling transformers and transmission line transformers. Magnetic coupling transformers utilize a magnetic field to transfer energy between circuits, while transmission line transformers use impedance matching to efficiently transfer signals between circuits. Both types play a critical role in RF applications by enabling the efficient transfer of radio frequency signals with minimal loss and distortion. Magnetic coupling transformers are commonly used in audio applications, while transmission line transformers are often used in high-frequency communication systems.</p></p>
