<p><strong>The IoT Ultrasonic Smart Gas Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transit Time Type</li><li>Doppler Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transit Time Type Ultrasonic Smart Gas Meters measure the time it takes for ultrasonic pulses to travel between two points, detecting gas flow by calculating the difference in time. Doppler Type Ultrasonic Smart Gas Meters use Doppler effect to measure gas flow, where the frequency shift in ultrasonic waves is proportional to the gas flow velocity. Both types offer accurate and efficient gas flow measurement in IoT applications, providing real-time data for monitoring and optimization of gas consumption.</p></p>
