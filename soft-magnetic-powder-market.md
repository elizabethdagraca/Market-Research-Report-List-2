<p><p>The soft magnetic powder market can be classified into three main types: pure iron powder, soft alloy powder, and other powders. The "Other" category includes a variety of specialized powders with unique compositions and properties, catering to specific industrial applications within the soft magnetic materials sector.</p></p>
