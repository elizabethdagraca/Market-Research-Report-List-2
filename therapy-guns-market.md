<p><p>Therapy guns are categorized into three main types based on their revolutions per minute (RPM) - 2400 RPM, 2500 RPM, and 3200 RPM. The RPM indicates the speed at which the therapy gun delivers percussive therapy to the muscles. The higher the RPM, the more intense and deeper the massage penetration. Therapy guns with 2400 RPM are ideal for sensitive areas, while 3200 RPM guns are better suited for deep tissue massage. The 2500 RPM guns offer a balance between intensity and comfort for most users.</p></p>
