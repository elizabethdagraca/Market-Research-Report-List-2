<p><ul><li>Bus Side</li><li>Line Side</li><li>Transfer Bus Side</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Double Break Isolator is a device used in electrical substations to physically disconnect electrical lines for maintenance or repairs. The three main types of Double Break Isolator markets are Bus Side, Line Side, and Transfer Bus Side. Bus Side isolators are connected to the busbar, Line Side isolators are connected to the transmission line, and Transfer Bus Side isolators are used to transfer a line from one busbar to another. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring safety and efficiency in electrical power systems.</p></p>
