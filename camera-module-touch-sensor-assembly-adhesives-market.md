<p><p>Camera module & touch sensor assembly adhesives market include UV-curable type and non-UV curable type adhesives. UV-curable adhesives are cured by exposure to ultraviolet light, providing fast bonding and high strength. Non-UV curable adhesives do not require UV light for curing and are commonly used for applications where UV light is not suitable. Both types offer strong adhesion properties and are essential for assembling camera modules and touch sensors in electronic devices.</p></p>
