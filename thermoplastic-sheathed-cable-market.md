<p><p>Thermoplastic-sheathed cable is a type of electrical cable that is insulated by a thermoplastic sheathing. There are two main types of thermoplastic-sheathed cable on the market: flat and round. Flat thermoplastic-sheathed cable is typically used in applications where space is limited, such as in buildings with shallow wall cavities. Round thermoplastic-sheathed cable is more commonly used in larger spaces and is easier to install due to its flexibility. Both types of cable offer similar levels of protection and durability, making them suitable for a variety of electrical wiring applications.</p></p>
