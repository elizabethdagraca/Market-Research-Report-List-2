<p><p>Warehouse rack market consists of various types such as Drive-In Pallet Racks, Push Back Pallet Racks, Cantilever Racks, and others. Drive-In Pallet Racks are designed for high-density storage with limited selectivity. Push Back Pallet Racks offer more selectivity and efficiency by utilizing a gravity flow system. Cantilever Racks are ideal for storing long and bulky items. Other types of racks include carton flow racks, mezzanine racks, and pallet flow racks, each offering unique storage solutions for different warehouse needs.</p></p>
