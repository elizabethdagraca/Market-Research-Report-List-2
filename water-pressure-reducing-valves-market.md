<p><p>Water pressure reducing valves are used to regulate the pressure of water flowing through pipes. There are two main types of pressure reducing valves: Direct acting type and Pilot operated type. Direct acting valves use a diaphragm to directly control the pressure, while Pilot operated valves use a separate control system to monitor and adjust the pressure. Both types are essential in maintaining a consistent and safe water pressure in residential, commercial and industrial applications.</p></p>
