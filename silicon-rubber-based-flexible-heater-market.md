<p><p>Silicon rubber-based flexible heaters come in various types to cater to different heating needs. The Rectangle Type heaters are designed with a flat shape, making them suitable for applications requiring even heat distribution over a large area. On the other hand, Round Type heaters are ideal for applications where the heating needs to be concentrated in a specific circular area. Both types of heaters offer flexibility, durability, and efficient heat transfer, making them popular choices in industries such as electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
