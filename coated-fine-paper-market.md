<p><p>Coated Fine Paper market consists of two main types: gloss-coated paper and dull-coated paper. Gloss-coated paper has a shiny, reflective surface that enhances colors and images, making it ideal for high-quality printing projects such as magazines and brochures. On the other hand, dull-coated paper has a more subtle, matte finish which reduces glare and is often used for textbooks, annual reports, and other materials where readability is key. Both types offer a professional look and feel, catering to different printing needs.</p></p>
