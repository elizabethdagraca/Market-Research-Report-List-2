<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Laminated Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Pressure Laminate (HPL)</li><li>Continuous Pressure Laminate (CPL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-Pressure Laminate (HPL) panels are created by saturating multiple layers of kraft paper with resin and fusing them together using high heat and pressure. This process produces a durable and versatile panel suitable for a wide range of applications. On the other hand, Continuous Pressure Laminate (CPL) panels are manufactured by continuously pressing resin-impregnated decorative papers onto a substrate under high pressure and heat. This results in a cost-effective and high-quality laminate panel option for various surfaces and furniture.</p></p>
