<p><p>Holographic projection film is classified into two main types: front projection film and rear projection film. Front projection film is designed to be viewed from the front, where the image is projected onto the film from a projector located in front of the screen. Rear projection film, on the other hand, is designed to be viewed from the rear, where the image is projected onto the film from behind the screen. Both types of projection films offer high-quality holographic displays for various applications.</p></p>
