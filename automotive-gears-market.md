<p><p>The automotive gears market consists of different types such as Spur Gear, Helical Gear, Bevel Gear, Worm Gear, Gear Rack, and Others. Spur gears have straight teeth and are commonly used in applications requiring high durability. Helical gears have angled teeth for smoother operation. Bevel gears are used to change the direction of power transmission. Worm gears are used for high torque applications. Gear racks are used in linear motion systems. The "Others" category includes various specialized gear types used in automotive applications.</p></p>
