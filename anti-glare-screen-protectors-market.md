<p><p>Anti-glare screen protectors come in two main types: single layer and multi-layer. Single layer screen protectors offer basic protection against glare by using a single anti-glare coating on the surface of the screen. On the other hand, multi-layer screen protectors provide advanced protection by incorporating multiple layers, such as anti-glare, anti-fingerprint, and shatterproof layers. Multi-layer screen protectors offer a higher level of protection and functionality compared to single layer screen protectors, making them a popular choice among consumers.</p></p>
