<p><strong>The Synthetic Resin Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Type</li><li>Water Type</li><li>Solvent-free Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Synthetic resin coatings come in three main types based on their formulation: Solvent Type coatings use organic solvents as a carrier, Water Type coatings use water as a carrier, and Solvent-free Type coatings do not contain any solvents. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of application, durability, and environmental impact. Solvent Type coatings offer fast drying and good adhesion, Water Type coatings are eco-friendly and easy to clean up, and Solvent-free Type coatings have low VOC emissions and are safer for workers.</p></p>
