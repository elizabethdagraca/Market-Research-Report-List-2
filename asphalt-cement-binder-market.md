<p><p>Asphalt cement binder market includes various types such as modified asphalt and impregnated asphalt. Modified asphalt is made by mixing asphalt with polymers or other additives to enhance its performance and durability. Impregnated asphalt, on the other hand, involves saturating a porous aggregate with asphalt to improve its binding capabilities. Both types of asphalt offer unique benefits and properties that make them suitable for different applications in the construction and road paving industries.</p></p>
