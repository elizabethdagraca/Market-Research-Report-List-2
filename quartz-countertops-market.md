<p><p>Quartz countertops market types refer to different manufacturing processes used to create quartz countertops. Press molding involves pressing quartz particles together using high pressure and heat, resulting in a durable and stylish countertop. On the other hand, casting molding involves pouring liquid resin and quartz mixtures into molds to create unique shapes and designs. Both methods offer a wide variety of options for consumers looking to enhance their kitchen or bathroom with quartz countertops.</p></p>
