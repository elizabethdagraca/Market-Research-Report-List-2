<p><p>Non-adhesive foam dressings are available in two main types: without adhesive border and with adhesive border. Non-adhesive foam dressings without adhesive border are suitable for wounds with minimal exudate and can be secured with tape or bandages. On the other hand, non-adhesive foam dressings with adhesive border are self-adhesive and can easily stick to the skin surrounding the wound, providing a secure and leak-proof seal. Both types offer a comfortable and effective solution for managing wounds.</p></p>
