<p><ul><li>2D</li><li>2.5D</li><li>3D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass interposers are used in semiconductor packaging to provide electrical connections between chips. In 2D interposers, electrical connections are made on a single layer of glass, while in 2.5D interposers, connections are made on multiple layers. 3D interposers have connections on multiple stacked layers of glass, allowing for greater flexibility and performance. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of scalability, performance, and cost. Ultimately, the choice of interposer type depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
