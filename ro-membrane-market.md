<p><p>Composite membranes are a type of RO membrane that consists of multiple layers of materials with different filtration properties, providing enhanced water purification capabilities. Asymmetric membranes, on the other hand, have varying pore sizes throughout the membrane structure, allowing for more efficient filtration of different sized particles. Both types of membranes are commonly used in the water treatment industry for desalination, wastewater treatment, and other filtration processes due to their high performance and durability.</p></p>
