<p><p>Industrial Foundry Additives Market encompasses three main types: Organic Additives, Inorganic Additives, and Hybrid Additives. Organic additives are derived from natural sources and are typically used for improving sand properties. Inorganic additives are made from minerals and metals and are known for enhancing the physical and mechanical properties of sand molds. Hybrid additives combine organic and inorganic components to create customized solutions for specific foundry applications, offering a balance between cost, performance, and sustainability. Each type of additive plays a crucial role in the foundry industry.</p></p>
