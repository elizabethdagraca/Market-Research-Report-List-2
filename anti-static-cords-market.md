<p><ul><li>Standard Anti-Static Cord</li><li>Elasticated Anti-Static Cord</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-static cords are used to prevent the buildup of static electricity during electronic assembly and testing. The standard anti-static cord is a straight, non-elastic cord that connects to grounding points to dissipate static charges. On the other hand, the elasticated anti-static cord is a stretchable cord that provides more flexibility and reach during use. Both types of anti-static cords are essential tools in electrostatic discharge protection, ensuring the safe handling of electronic components.</p></p>
