<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solventborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Furniture varnish market consists of two main types: waterborne and solventborne. Waterborne varnishes are eco-friendly, easy to clean up, and emit less harmful fumes compared to solventborne varnishes. On the other hand, solventborne varnishes are more durable and offer better protection against wear and tear. While waterborne varnishes are preferred for indoor furniture due to their low toxicity and odor, solventborne varnishes are commonly used for outdoor furniture or high-traffic areas where durability is a priority.</p></p>
