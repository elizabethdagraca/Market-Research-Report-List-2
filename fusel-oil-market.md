Extraction distillation involves separating fusel oil by distilling it along with the main product. Membrane separation, on the other hand, uses membranes to separate fusel oil from the main product. Both methods have their own market types, with extraction distillation being more commonly used in certain industries, while membrane separation is gaining popularity due to its efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
