<p><p>The precast concrete stairs market includes two main types: beam-type stairs and slab stairs. Beam-type stairs are prefabricated with a central beam supporting the treads and risers, providing structural integrity. Slab stairs, on the other hand, are made of solid precast concrete slabs that are installed without the need for additional structural support. Both types offer quick and cost-effective installation, durable construction, and a wide range of design options, making them popular choices for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.</p></p>
