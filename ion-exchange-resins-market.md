<p><ul><li>Anion Exchange Resin</li><li>Cation Exchange Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ion exchange resins market can be categorized into two main types: anion exchange resins and cation exchange resins. Anion exchange resins are utilized to remove negatively charged ions from solutions, while cation exchange resins are used to remove positively charged ions. Both types of resins play a crucial role in various industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and chemical processing. They help in purification, separation, and decontamination processes by exchanging ions in solution with ions on the resin surface.</p></p>
