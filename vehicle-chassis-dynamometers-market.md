<p><strong>The Vehicle Chassis Dynamometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Roller Type</li><li>Multi Roller Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Roller Type chassis dynamometers consist of a single roller on which the vehicle's drive wheels are placed for testing. This type of dynamometer is more compact and cost-effective, making it ideal for smaller testing facilities or for individual vehicle testing. </p><p>On the other hand, Multi Roller Type chassis dynamometers feature multiple rollers that allow for more accurate and dynamic testing of a vehicle's performance. This type of dynamometer is often used in larger testing facilities or by manufacturers to test multiple vehicles simultaneously.</p></p>
