<p><ul><li>Frontal Airbags</li><li>Side Airbags</li><li>Curtain Airbags</li><li>Knee Airbags</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Frontal airbags are typically located in the front of the vehicle and are designed to protect the driver and front passenger in the event of a frontal collision. Side airbags are located in the doors or seats and provide protection for occupants in the event of a side impact. Curtain airbags deploy from the ceiling and provide additional protection for occupants in the front and rear seats. Knee airbags are located in the lower dashboard area and provide protection for the driver's knees. Other types of airbags may include seat-mounted airbags or inflatable seatbelts.</p></p>
