<p><p>Chemicals packaging market types include sacks, drums, FIBC (Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers), and other options. Sacks are commonly used for powdered or granular chemicals due to their convenience and ease of handling. Drums are preferred for liquids and hazardous chemicals as they offer higher safety and durability. FIBCs are large bags used for bulk quantities of chemicals, providing cost-effective and efficient transportation and storage. Other packaging options such as boxes, cartons, and bottles are also used depending on the specific requirements of the chemical product.</p></p>
