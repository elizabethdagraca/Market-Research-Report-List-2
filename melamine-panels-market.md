<p><p>Melamine panels are widely used in the market due to their durability and aesthetic appeal. There are two main types of melamine panels: Particle panels and Density panels. Particle panels are made of wood particles mixed with resin and pressed together, providing a cost-effective option for furniture and cabinetry. Density panels are made of high-density fiberboard coated with a melamine finish, offering a smoother surface and enhanced durability. Both types cater to different market needs, providing versatile options for various applications.</p></p>
