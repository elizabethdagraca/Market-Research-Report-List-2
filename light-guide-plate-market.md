<p><ul><li>Edge LGP</li><li>Bottom LGP</li><li>Printed LGP</li><li>Non-printed LGP</li><li>Injection Molding LGP</li><li>Cut Forming LGP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Light Guide Plate market is divided into various types based on their design and manufacturing process. Edge LGP features light guiding on the edges, while Bottom LGP distributes light uniformly from the back. Printed LGP has graphics or text printed on the surface, whereas Non-printed LGP does not have any graphics. Injection Molding LGP is manufactured through injection molding process, and Cut Forming LGP is produced by cutting and forming sheets of material. Each type caters to different lighting applications and requirements.</p></p>
