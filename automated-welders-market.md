Projection welding involves applying pressure and heat to join two metal parts together, while spot welding uses electrodes to pass current through the parts, creating a weld. Both types offer efficient and precise welding solutions for different applications, making them preferred choices in manufacturing processes where speed, accuracy, and consistency are crucial.</p></p>
