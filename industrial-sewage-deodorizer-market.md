<p><p>Industrial sewage deodorizers come in two main types: solid and liquid. Solid deodorizers are typically in the form of granules or blocks that are placed directly in the sewage system to neutralize odors. Liquid deodorizers are mixed with water and sprayed or poured into the sewage system to achieve the same effect. Both solid and liquid deodorizers are commonly used in industrial settings to control unpleasant odors and maintain a hygienic environment.</p></p>
