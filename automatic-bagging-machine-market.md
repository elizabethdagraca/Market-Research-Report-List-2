<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic Bagging Machine</li><li>Semi-Automatic Bagging Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic bagging machines come in two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic machines are capable of performing all bagging tasks with little to no human intervention, making them ideal for high-volume production lines. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of human input during the bagging process, such as manually placing the product in the bag or sealing the bag. Both types of machines offer efficiency and convenience in different levels depending on the production needs of a business.</p></p>
