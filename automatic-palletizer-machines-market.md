<p><p>Automatic Palletizer Machines are commonly categorized into two main types: Robotic Type and Mechanical Type. Robotic Type palletizers are equipped with robotic arms to pick, place, and stack products onto pallets automatically. This type offers increased flexibility and efficiency in handling various types of products. On the other hand, Mechanical Type palletizers use mechanical components such as conveyors and pushers to accomplish the same tasks. While less flexible than Robotic Type machines, Mechanical Type palletizers are more cost-effective and suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
