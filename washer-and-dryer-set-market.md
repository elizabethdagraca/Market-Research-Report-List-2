<p><p>Washer and dryer sets can be categorized into two main types: stacked and side by side. Stacked sets are designed to save space by stacking the dryer on top of the washer, making them ideal for small laundry rooms or apartments. Both types have their own advantages and are popular choices depending on the available space and layout of the laundry area.</p></p>
