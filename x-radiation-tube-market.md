<p><strong>The X-Radiation Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>X-Radiation Tube Market includes two main types: Stationary Anode and Rotating Anode. Stationary Anode tubes have a fixed target that is bombarded with electrons to produce x-rays, making them suitable for low to moderate usage applications. Rotating Anode tubes have a rotating target that can handle high heat levels and continuous usage, making them ideal for high-intensity imaging procedures. Both types offer specific advantages and are used in different medical and industrial x-ray applications based on their capabilities and performance.</p></p>
