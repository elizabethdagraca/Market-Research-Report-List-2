<p><strong>The Magnetron Sputtering Targets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Magnetron Sputtering</li><li>RF Magnetron Sputtering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetron sputtering targets market consists of two main types: DC magnetron sputtering and RF magnetron sputtering. DC magnetron sputtering involves the application of a direct current to generate a plasma discharge, while RF magnetron sputtering uses radio frequency power for plasma generation. Both methods are widely used in the deposition of thin films on various materials. DC magnetron sputtering is preferred for high deposition rates and thick film growth, while RF magnetron sputtering is chosen for its better control over film properties and lower substrate heating.</p></p>
