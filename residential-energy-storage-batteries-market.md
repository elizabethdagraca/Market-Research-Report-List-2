<p><p>Residential energy storage batteries are used in both grid-tied and off-grid applications. In grid-tied systems, the batteries work in conjunction with the utility grid to store excess energy generated from renewable sources and provide backup power during outages. In off-grid systems, the batteries are the primary source of power, storing energy generated from sources like solar panels or wind turbines for use when the grid is not available. Both applications contribute to increased energy efficiency, reliability, and sustainability in residential settings.</p></p>
