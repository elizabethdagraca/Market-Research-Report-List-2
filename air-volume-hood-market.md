There are two types of air volume hood markets: Bracket Type and Positive Displacement. Bracket Type hoods are attached to the air outlet, while Positive Displacement hoods use a mechanism to displace air and calculate volume. Both types are essential tools for ensuring proper air ventilation and circulation in buildings and industrial settings.</p></p>
