Signal Converters are used to amplify and convert the weak signal from a thermocouple into a stronger, more reliable output signal. On the other hand, Digital Converters are used to convert the analog signal from a thermocouple into a digital signal for easier processing and analysis. Both types of converters play a crucial role in accurately measuring temperature in various industrial processes.</p></p>
