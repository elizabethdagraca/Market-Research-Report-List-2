<p><p>High-efficiency solar modules such as HJT cells and IBC cells are two types of advanced solar technology that offer higher efficiency in converting sunlight into electricity. HJT cells, or heterojunction cells, utilize multiple layers of materials to improve energy conversion rates. IBC cells, or interdigitated back contact cells, have electrodes on the back to reduce energy loss. These types of solar modules are becoming increasingly popular in the market due to their ability to generate more electricity from the same amount of sunlight.</p></p>
