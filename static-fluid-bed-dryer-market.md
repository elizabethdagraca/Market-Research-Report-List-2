<p><p>Static fluid bed dryers are categorized into two main types: continuous type and batch type. Continuous type dryers are designed for large-scale production processes where materials are continuously fed into the dryer and dried in a continuous flow. On the other hand, batch type dryers are suited for smaller-scale operations where materials are loaded into the dryer in batches and dried in separate, distinct cycles. Both types of dryers offer efficient and effective drying solutions for various industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food processing.</p></p>
