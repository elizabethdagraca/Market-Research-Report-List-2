<p><ul><li>Rocker Switches</li><li>Snap Action Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rocker switches and snap action switches are two common types of trigger switches used in various industries. Rocker switches have a lever that rocks back and forth to open or close the circuit, making them easy to operate and widely used in household appliances. Snap action switches, on the other hand, have a spring mechanism that rapidly snaps the contacts open or closed when activated, providing quick and reliable switching in applications requiring precise and immediate response. Both types cater to different market needs based on their design and functionality.</p></p>
