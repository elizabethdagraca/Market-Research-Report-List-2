<p><ul><li>High Boron Glass Lamp</li><li>Quartz Glass Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>UV light resources market can be categorized into two main types: High Boron Glass Lamp and Quartz Glass Lamp. High Boron Glass Lamp utilizes borosilicate glass to filter out harmful infrared radiation while allowing UV light to pass through. On the other hand, Quartz Glass Lamp is made from high purity quartz material, which is resistant to high temperatures and provides a more efficient transmission of UV light. Both types of lamps are commonly used in various applications such as water purification, air sterilization, and curing processes in industries.</p></p>
