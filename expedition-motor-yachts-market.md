<p><strong>The Expedition Motor Yachts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Displacement Hull</li><li>Semi-Displacement Hull</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Expedition motor yachts are typically classified into two main types based on their hull design: displacement hull and semi-displacement hull. </p><p>Displacement hull expedition motor yachts are known for their full keels and rounded hull shapes, providing stability and fuel efficiency at lower speeds. These yachts are designed for long-range cruising and are favored for their comfortable ride.</p><p>On the other hand, semi-displacement hull expedition motor yachts have a flatter hull shape, allowing for higher speeds while still providing good fuel efficiency. They are versatile vessels suitable for both cruising and occasional higher speeds.</p></p>
