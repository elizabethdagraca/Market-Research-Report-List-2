<p><strong>The Antifouling Paint Remover Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Use</li><li>Use After Water Dissolving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antifouling paint removers are typically categorized into two main market types - direct use and use after water dissolving. Direct use antifouling paint removers are applied directly onto the surface without any additional steps, making them convenient for quick and easy removal of paint. On the other hand, use after water dissolving products require the paint to be soaked in water before applying the remover, allowing for a more thorough and effective removal process. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
