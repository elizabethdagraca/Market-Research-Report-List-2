<p><p>Bio-based coolants are classified into two main types- semi-synthetic and synthetic. Semi-synthetic bio-based coolants are a blend of traditional coolants and bio-based ingredients, offering a more environmentally friendly option while still maintaining some synthetic properties. On the other hand, synthetic bio-based coolants are fully derived from renewable sources and are completely free from petroleum-based chemicals. Both types of bio-based coolants are gaining popularity in the market as consumers increasingly seek sustainable and eco-friendly alternatives to traditional coolant options.</p></p>
