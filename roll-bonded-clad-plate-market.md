<p><p>Roll Bonded Clad Plate market includes two main types: Single Side Cladded and Double Side Cladded. In Single Side Cladded market, only one side of the base metal is overlaid with a clad material, providing benefits such as cost-effectiveness and corrosion resistance. On the other hand, in Double Side Cladded market, both sides of the base metal are covered with a clad material, offering superior durability and strength. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and requirements.</p></p>
