<p><p>Industrial Edge Controllers are commonly categorized into two market types - DIN-rail and Panel Mount. DIN-rail controllers are designed to be mounted on a standard DIN rail inside a control panel, offering easy integration and space-saving capabilities. Panel Mount controllers, on the other hand, are designed to be mounted directly onto a flat surface, providing flexibility in installation options. Both types cater to different industrial needs and preferences, offering varied benefits in terms of space utilization, installation ease, and overall functionality in industrial automation applications.</p></p>
