<p><p>The 3D Automotive Camera Module market includes two main types of camera technologies: Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor (CMOS) and Charge-Coupled Device (CCD). CMOS cameras are known for their low power consumption, high integration capabilities, and fast readout speeds, making them ideal for automotive applications. On the other hand, CCD cameras offer superior image quality and low noise performance, making them suitable for high-end automotive cameras. Both technologies play a crucial role in providing advanced driver assistance systems and enhancing overall vehicle safety.</p></p>
