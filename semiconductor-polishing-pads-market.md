<p><p>Semiconductor polishing pads market is divided into two main types: Hard CMP Pads and Soft CMP Pads. Hard CMP Pads are designed for aggressive material removal and provide high durability, making them suitable for rough polishing processes. On the other hand, Soft CMP Pads are used for less aggressive material removal and offer softer touch, making them ideal for fine polishing applications. Both types of pads play a crucial role in the semiconductor manufacturing process by ensuring optimal surface smoothness and quality.</p></p>
