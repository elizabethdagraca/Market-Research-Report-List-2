<p><p>Honeycomb sandwich panels are lightweight structures composed of a core material, typically made of aluminum, aramid, or thermoplastic, sandwiched between two layers of face sheets. Each type of core material offers distinct characteristics and benefits, making them suitable for various applications. Aluminum honeycomb panels provide excellent strength-to-weight ratio and are commonly used in aerospace and transportation industries. Aramid honeycomb panels offer high impact resistance and are used in defense and marine applications. Thermoplastic honeycomb panels are known for their flexibility and are used in architectural and automotive industries.</p></p>
