<p><p>Radial shaft seals are used to prevent the leakage of lubricants and other fluids in rotating machinery. Single lip shaft seals have one sealing lip that comes into contact with the shaft, providing basic sealing protection. On the other hand, double lip shaft seals have two sealing lips for enhanced protection against leaks. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and mining to ensure the efficient performance and longevity of equipment.</p></p>
