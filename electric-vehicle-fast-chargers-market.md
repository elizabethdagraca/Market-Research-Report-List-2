<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Fast Chargers market consists of two main types: On-board Electric Vehicle Charger (EVC) and Off-board Electric Vehicle Charger (EVC). On-board EVCs are integrated into the electric vehicle itself and are used for charging the vehicle's battery while it is in motion. Off-board EVCs, on the other hand, are standalone charging stations located outside the vehicle that are used for quickly charging the electric vehicle's battery when parked. Both types play a crucial role in supporting the growth of electric vehicles by providing fast and convenient charging solutions.</p></p>
