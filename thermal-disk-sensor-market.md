<p><p>A Thermal Disk Sensor is a type of temperature sensor that utilizes a bimetallic disk to measure temperature changes. When the temperature of the disk changes, it causes the bimetallic strip to bend, which in turn activates a switch or transmits a signal to indicate the temperature change. These sensors are commonly used in various applications such as HVAC systems, automotive industry, and industrial processes.</p><p>The Thermal Disk Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.70% during the forecast period.
