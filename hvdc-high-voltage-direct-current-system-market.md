<p><strong>The HVDC (High Voltage Direct Current) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-terminal HVDC System</li><li>Multi-terminal HVDC System (MTDC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVDC (High Voltage Direct Current) System market includes two main types: two-terminal HVDC system and multi-terminal HVDC system (MTDC). Two-terminal HVDC system involves a point-to-point connection between two converter stations, enabling efficient long-distance transmission of power. On the other hand, MTDC system involves multiple converter stations connected in a network configuration, allowing for more flexible and reliable power transmission across a wider area. Both types of HVDC systems play a crucial role in enhancing grid stability and integrating renewable energy sources into the power grid.</p></p>
