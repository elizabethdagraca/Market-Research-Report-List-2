<p><p>Gas permeability testers are used to measure the permeability of gases through various materials. Different types of gas permeability testers include oxygen permeability testers, water vapor permeability testers, air permeability testers, and others. Oxygen permeability testers specifically measure the rate at which oxygen passes through a material, while water vapor permeability testers measure the rate of water vapor transmission. Air permeability testers measure the ability of materials to allow air to pass through. Other types of gas permeability testers may be designed to measure specific gases or combinations of gases.</p></p>
