<p><p>Soft carbon anode materials for lithium-ion batteries are primarily sourced from petroleum coke and needle coke. Other sources include mesophase carbon materials and synthetic graphite. Each type of source material offers different properties and performance characteristics, influencing their suitability for specific battery applications. As demand for lithium-ion batteries continues to grow, the market for soft carbon anode materials sourced from petroleum coke, needle coke, and other materials is expected to expand.</p></p>
