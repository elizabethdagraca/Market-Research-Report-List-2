UV curable adhesives are fast-curing and offer high bond strength, making them ideal for applications requiring quick assembly. Non-UV curable adhesives, on the other hand, are suitable for bonding materials that are sensitive to UV light or for applications where UV lamps are not feasible. Both types of adhesives play a crucial role in the assembly of camera modules, ensuring reliable and durable bonding.</p></p>
