<p><p>Ice and Snow Melter market consists of two main types: Liquid and Solid. Liquid ice and snow melters are typically in the form of brine solutions or calcium chloride, which are applied directly onto icy surfaces to quickly melt the ice and snow. Solid ice and snow melters, on the other hand, are granular or pellet forms of materials such as rock salt or magnesium chloride that are spread over icy surfaces to help break down and melt the ice. Both types are effective in managing winter weather hazards.</p></p>
