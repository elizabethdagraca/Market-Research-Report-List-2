<p><p>Ceiling-Mounted Lighting refers to fixtures that are installed on the ceiling to provide overhead illumination. The three main types of ceiling-mounted lighting are Flush Mount, Semi Flush, and Other. Flush Mount fixtures are installed directly onto the ceiling, while Semi Flush fixtures are hung slightly lower, allowing for more space between the fixture and the ceiling. Other types of ceiling-mounted lighting include chandeliers, pendant lights, and track lighting. These fixtures offer various design options and can add both functionality and style to a space.</p></p>
