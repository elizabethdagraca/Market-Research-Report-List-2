<p><p>Mineral wool roof insulation includes two main types: rockwool and glass wool. Rockwool roof insulation is made from mineral fibers derived from basalt rock, providing excellent thermal and acoustic insulation properties. Glass wool roof insulation, on the other hand, is made from recycled glass and offers similar benefits in terms of insulation. Both types are popular choices for roof insulation due to their fire resistance, durability, and cost-effectiveness. Their unique compositions make them effective options for regulating indoor temperatures and reducing energy costs.</p></p>
