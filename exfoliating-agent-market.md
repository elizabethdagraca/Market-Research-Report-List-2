<p><ul><li>Physical Exfoliating Agent</li><li>Chemical Exfoliating Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exfoliating agents are categorized into physical and chemical types based on their mode of action. Physical exfoliating agents work by physically scrubbing away dead skin cells and impurities from the skin's surface. Common physical exfoliants include scrubs, brushes, and exfoliating mitts. On the other hand, chemical exfoliating agents use enzymes or acids to dissolve dead skin cells and promote cell turnover. Examples of chemical exfoliants include AHAs (alpha hydroxy acids) and BHAs (beta hydroxy acids). Both types of exfoliating agents help improve skin texture and appearance.</p></p>
