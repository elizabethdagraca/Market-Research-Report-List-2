<p><ul><li>Single-stage Hydraulic Torque Converter</li><li>Multistage Hydraulic Torque Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic torque converter market consists of two main types: Single-stage and Multistage hydraulic torque converters. Single-stage hydraulic torque converters are simpler in design and use only one set of blades to transfer torque from the engine to the transmission. On the other hand, Multistage hydraulic torque converters are more complex and use multiple sets of blades to achieve higher torque multiplication. They are often used in heavy-duty machinery and vehicles that require more power and torque transfer capabilities.</p></p>
