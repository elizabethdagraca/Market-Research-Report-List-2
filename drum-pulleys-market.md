<p><p>Drum pulleys are classified into two main types: support pulleys and drive pulleys. Support pulleys are used to provide support and guidance to the belt in a conveyor system, helping to maintain proper alignment and tension. Drive pulleys, on the other hand, are responsible for transferring power from the motor to the belt, driving the movement of the conveyor system. Both types of pulleys play a crucial role in the efficiency and performance of belt conveyor systems in various industries.</p></p>
