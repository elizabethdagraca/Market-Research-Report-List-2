<p><p>The low-voltage circuit breaker market consists of three main types: Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breaker (ACB). MCBs are commonly used in residential and commercial settings for protection against overcurrents and short circuits. MCCBs are larger and used in industrial applications for higher currents. ACBs are designed for high-powered applications and provide protection against overloads and short circuits. Each type of circuit breaker serves a specific purpose in safeguarding electrical systems and equipment.</p></p>
