<p><p>PTFE resins are commonly produced using two main methods: dispersion method and suspension method. In the dispersion method, PTFE particles are dispersed in a liquid medium to form a paste, which is then processed and molded into different products. On the other hand, in the suspension method, PTFE particles are suspended in water and polymerized to form a resin. Both methods have their own advantages and are widely used in various industries for their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
