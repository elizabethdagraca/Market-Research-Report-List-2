<p><p>Dredging equipment market consists of various types of dredgers such as Mechanical Dredger, Hydraulic Dredger, and Other Dredger. Mechanical dredgers are typically used for shallow waters and are equipped with a rotating cutter head or bucket. Hydraulic dredgers use a suction pump to transport material, making them suitable for deep waters. Other dredgers include grab dredgers and backhoes, which are used for specialized tasks such as rock dredging or environmental projects. Each type of dredger offers unique capabilities and is chosen based on the specific dredging requirements.</p></p>
