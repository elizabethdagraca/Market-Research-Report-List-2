<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) market is segmented into Open Cell, Closed Cell, and Others. Open Cell SPF is soft and flexible, allowing for air permeability while providing sound insulation. Closed Cell SPF is rigid and dense, ideal for moisture resistance and structural reinforcement. The Others segment includes specialty SPF products tailored to specific applications such as roofing or insulation. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the construction industry, offering varying levels of insulation, air sealing, and structural support.</p></p>
