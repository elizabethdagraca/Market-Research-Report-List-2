<p><p>Automatic titration systems are widely used in laboratories for accurate and efficient analysis of samples. There are two main types of titration systems: coulometric titration and volumetric titration. Coulometric titration involves measuring the amount of electricity required to complete a reaction, while volumetric titration involves measuring the volume of a titrant needed to reach a certain endpoint. Both types of titration systems offer precise and reliable results, making them essential tools in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and environmental testing.</p></p>
