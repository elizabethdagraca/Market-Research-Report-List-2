<p><p>Pod Coffee Machines come in two market types: Closed Source System and Open Source System. Closed Source Systems use pods made by specific brands, limiting consumers' options and requiring them to purchase only from that brand. On the other hand, Open Source Systems allow consumers to use pods from various brands, offering more flexibility and customization in their coffee choices. Both systems have their advantages and disadvantages, catering to different preferences and needs of coffee lovers.</p></p>
