<p><ul><li>Alkaline Cleaners</li><li>Acid Cleaners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial metal cleaners can be classified into two main types: Alkaline Cleaners and Acid Cleaners. Alkaline cleaners are generally used for removing organic residues, oils, greases, and dirt from metal surfaces, offering high cleaning efficiency and relatively low cost. On the other hand, Acid cleaners are used for removing inorganic contaminants, such as rust, scale, and oxides, from metal surfaces. They are effective in removing tough stains and rust but tend to be more corrosive and require careful handling.</p></p>
