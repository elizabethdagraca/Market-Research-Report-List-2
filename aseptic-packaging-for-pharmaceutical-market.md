<p><p>Aseptic packaging in the pharmaceutical market typically involves the use of glass, plastic, or other materials to maintain the sterility of products. Glass containers are commonly used for parenteral packaging due to their impermeability and inertness. Plastic packaging is popular for oral medications and other non-injectable products due to its cost-effectiveness and flexibility. Other materials such as aluminum are also used in aseptic packaging for their barrier properties. Each material type is chosen based on the specific requirements of the pharmaceutical product being packaged.</p></p>
