<p><p>Three-point hitch cultivators come in different market types such as hanging cultivators and semi-mounted cultivators. Hanging cultivators are designed to be mounted directly onto the tractor's three-point hitch, making them easily adjustable and maneuverable. On the other hand, semi-mounted cultivators are attached to a frame that is supported by the tractor's rear wheels, providing more stability and weight distribution for heavier duty cultivation tasks. Both types of cultivators offer unique advantages depending on the specific needs and preferences of the user.</p></p>
