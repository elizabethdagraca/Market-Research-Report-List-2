Single-use bioreactors are disposable systems that reduce the risk of contamination and cleaning costs, making them ideal for small-scale production. Multiple-use bioreactors, on the other hand, are reusable systems that are more cost-effective for large-scale production. Both types play a crucial role in bioprocessing and are selected based on the scale and requirements of the specific application.</p></p>
