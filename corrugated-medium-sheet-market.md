<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Corrugated Medium Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A Flute</li><li>B Flute</li><li>C Flute</li><li>D Flute</li><li>E Flute</li><li>F Flute</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrugated medium sheets are classified based on the size and shape of the flutes, which are the S-shaped waves that provide strength and cushioning in the material. A Flute is the largest and provides excellent stacking strength, while B Flute is smaller and offers a smoother printing surface. C Flute is the most common type, balancing strength and flexibility. D Flute is even smaller and ideal for retail packaging. E Flute is very thin and lightweight, making it suitable for small retail items. F Flute is the smallest and used for intricate die-cut designs.</p></p>
