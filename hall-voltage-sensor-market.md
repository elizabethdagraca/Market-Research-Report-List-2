<p><p>Hall voltage sensors can be classified into two main types: Analog Output Hall Sensors and Digital Output Hall Sensors.</p><p>Analog Output Hall Sensors provide a continuous voltage output signal proportional to the magnetic field strength, allowing for precise measurements of the field. Digital Output Hall Sensors, on the other hand, convert the magnetic field strength into a digital signal for easy processing and integration with digital systems.</p><p>Both types cater to different applications and industries, offering unique benefits and capabilities based on the output signal they provide.</p></p>
