<p><p>Birefringent tuners are devices used to manipulate the polarization of light in optical systems. Single-plate tuners use a single birefringent crystal plate to achieve polarization control, while multi-plate tuners use multiple plates for more precise tuning capabilities. Single-plate tuners are simpler and more cost-effective, while multi-plate tuners offer higher performance and precision. Both types are used in various applications such as spectroscopy, telecommunications, and laser systems to control the polarization of light for optimal performance.</p></p>
