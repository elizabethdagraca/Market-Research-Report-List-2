<p><p>Portable X-ray and CT scan devices are categorized into two types: One-Piece Type and Split Type. One-Piece Type devices are all-in-one units that incorporate the X-ray tube, generator, and detector in a single portable unit. Split Type devices, on the other hand, consist of separate components that can be connected for use in different locations. One-Piece Type devices are more compact and easier to transport, while Split Type devices offer more flexibility in terms of positioning and use in different settings.</p></p>
