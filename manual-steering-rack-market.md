<p><strong>The Manual Steering Rack Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rack and Pinion Type</li><li>Ball Screw Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual steering rack market includes two main types: Rack and Pinion Type and Ball Screw Type. Rack and Pinion Type steering racks use a circular gear (the rack) that rotates to turn the wheels when the driver turns the steering wheel. Ball Screw Type steering racks use a threaded shaft (the ball screw) and a set of bearings to convert rotational motion into linear motion to turn the wheels. Both types are common in manual steering systems in vehicles.</p></p>
