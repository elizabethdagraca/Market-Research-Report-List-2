<p><p>Aquaponics is a sustainable farming system that combines aquaculture (fish farming) and hydroponics (soilless plant cultivation) in a symbiotic environment. In an aquaponics system, the waste products from the fish are used as nutrients for the plants, while the plants help filter the water for the fish. This closed-loop system requires less water and land compared to traditional farming methods, making it a more environmentally friendly and efficient way to produce food.</p><p>The Aquaponics System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.7% during the forecast period. The market growth is driven by increasing demand for organic and locally grown produce, as well as growing awareness about sustainable farming practices.
