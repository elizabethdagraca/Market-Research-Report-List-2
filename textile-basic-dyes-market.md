<p><p>Textile Basic Dyes are available in two main forms - Liquid and Powder. The Liquid market consists of dyes that are pre-dissolved and ready to be used directly on fabrics. This form is convenient for quick application and mixing with other liquids. On the other hand, the Powder market offers dyes in dry form, which can be easily mixed with water or other solvents before application. This form is preferred for its longer shelf life and ease of transportation. Both forms offer a wide range of colors and are commonly used in textile dyeing processes.</p></p>
