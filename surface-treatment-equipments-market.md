<p><p>Surface treatment equipments are utilized in various industries to improve the adhesion of inks, adhesives, and coatings on surfaces. Plasma treatment equipments use ionized gas to clean and activate surfaces, enhancing adhesion properties. On the other hand, corona treatment equipments utilize high-voltage electrical discharge to modify the surface energy of materials, improving wettability and adhesion. Both types of equipments are essential in industries such as packaging, automotive, and electronics for ensuring proper adhesion and surface treatment of materials.</p></p>
