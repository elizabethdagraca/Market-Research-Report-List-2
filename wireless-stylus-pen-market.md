<p><strong>The Wireless Stylus Pen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Type</li><li>Passive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Active type wireless stylus pens require a power source and are equipped with electronic components to communicate with the device they are paired with. These pens typically offer more features such as pressure sensitivity and palm rejection. Passive type pens, on the other hand, do not require a power source and rely on the device's touch screen technology to function. They are simpler in design and are often used for basic navigation and drawing tasks. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences in the wireless stylus pen market.</p></p>
