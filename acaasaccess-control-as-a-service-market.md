<p><p>ACaaS (Access Control as a Service) market consists of two main types: Hosted and Managed services. </p><p>Hosted ACaaS involves the cloud-based delivery of access control solutions, where the service provider maintains and manages the access control system remotely. This type of service offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness to businesses.</p><p>On the other hand, Managed ACaaS involves outsourcing the management and maintenance of the access control system to a third-party service provider. This type of service ensures professional expertise, proactive monitoring, and support for businesses.</p></p>
