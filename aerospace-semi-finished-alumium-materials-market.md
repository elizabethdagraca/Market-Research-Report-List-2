<p><p>Aerospace semi-finished aluminum materials market consists of various types including rods, sheets, plates, and tubes. Rods are cylindrical bars that are commonly used in aerospace applications for their strength and durability. Sheets are thin, flat pieces of aluminum that are used for construction of aircraft structures. Plates are thicker and larger than sheets, often used for structural components. Tubes are hollow cylindrical aluminum pieces used in aerospace engineering for applications such as fuel lines and hydraulic systems. Each type of material serves a specific purpose in the aerospace industry.</p></p>
