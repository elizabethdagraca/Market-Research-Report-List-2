<p><p>Low finned tubes are specialized heat exchange tubes designed to increase surface area for improved heat transfer efficiency. There are two main types in the market: Single Metal Low Finned Tubes, which are made entirely of one material such as copper or stainless steel, and Poly Metal Low Finned Tubes, which have fins attached to the base tube made of a different metal for enhanced performance. Both types offer specific advantages in various applications, such as increased heat transfer rates and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
