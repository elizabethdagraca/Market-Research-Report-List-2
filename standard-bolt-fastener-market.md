<p><p>Standard bolts are commonly divided into two market types: half screw bolts and full screw bolts. Half screw bolts are partially threaded with a portion of the bolt left unthreaded, suitable for applications where a secure connection is needed but with some adjustability. Full screw bolts, on the other hand, are fully threaded from end to end, providing maximum grip and stability. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the fastener market, offering versatile solutions for various construction and industrial applications.</p></p>
