<p><p>Geomembrane liners are widely used in various industries for containment purposes. The market for geomembrane liners includes high-density polyethylene (HDPE), linear low-density polyethylene (LLDPE), polyvinyl chloride (PVC), flexible polypropylene (FPP), and other types of liners. HDPE liners are known for their durability and chemical resistance, while LLDPE liners are more flexible. PVC liners are cost-effective and easy to install. FPP liners offer excellent puncture resistance. Other types of liners may include materials such as EPDM, XR-5, and RPE.</p></p>
