<p><strong>The Sound Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Frequency</li><li>Low Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sound detectors can be categorized into high frequency and low frequency markets based on the range of frequencies they are able to detect. High frequency sound detectors are designed to pick up sounds with frequencies above the normal range of human hearing, typically above 20,000 Hz. These detectors are commonly used in ultrasonic applications such as medical imaging and pest control. In contrast, low frequency sound detectors are designed to detect sounds with frequencies below the normal range of human hearing, typically below 20 Hz. These detectors are commonly used in applications such as earthquake monitoring and underwater acoustics.</p></p>
