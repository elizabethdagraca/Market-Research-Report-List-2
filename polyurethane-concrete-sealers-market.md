<p><strong>The Polyurethane Concrete Sealers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based Polyurethane Concrete Sealers</li><li>Solvent-Based Polyurethane Concrete Sealers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane concrete sealers are available in two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based polyurethane sealers are environmentally friendly, easy to clean up, and emit low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs). On the other hand, solvent-based polyurethane sealers are more durable and provide a higher gloss finish. Both types of sealers offer protection against water, chemicals, and abrasion, making them ideal for a wide range of concrete surfaces such as driveways, patios, and floors.</p></p>
