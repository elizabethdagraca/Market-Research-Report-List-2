<p><strong>The Wireless Power Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers</li><li>Capacitive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inductive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers consist of coils that generate a magnetic field to transfer power wirelessly. They are commonly used in applications such as charging pads and electric toothbrushes. Capacitive Coupling Wireless Power Receivers use electric fields to transfer power, typically through capacitors. They are used in applications where a physical connection is not feasible or desirable, such as medical implants. Both types offer the convenience of wireless charging, but their specific design and functionality cater to different needs and applications.</p></p>
