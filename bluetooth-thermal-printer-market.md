<p><strong>The Bluetooth Thermal Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Thermal Thermal Printer</li><li>Thermal Transfer Thermal Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bluetooth Thermal Printer Market consists of two main types: Direct Thermal Thermal Printer and Thermal Transfer Thermal Printer. Direct Thermal printers use heat to produce an image on heat-sensitive paper without the need for ink or toner, making them ideal for receipts and labels. They are commonly used in retail and logistics sectors. Thermal Transfer printers, on the other hand, use a thermal print head to transfer ink from a ribbon onto paper, making them suitable for durable and long-lasting labels. They are often used in industries like healthcare and manufacturing.</p></p>
