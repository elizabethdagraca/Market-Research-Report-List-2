<p><p>Automatic trucks are classified into five levels based on their automation capabilities. Level 1 trucks have basic driver assistance features. Level 2 trucks can assist with steering, acceleration, and braking in certain circumstances. Level 3 trucks can manage driving tasks independently but require human intervention if needed. Level 4 trucks are fully autonomous but may still have manual controls. Level 5 trucks are fully autonomous with no need for human intervention, allowing for completely driverless operation.</p></p>
