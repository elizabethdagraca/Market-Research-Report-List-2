<p><p>Glass fiber complex materials are used in various industries for their high strength and durability. The market is divided into two types based on resin matrix: thermoset composites and thermoplastic composites. Thermoset composites are cured under heat and pressure, making them rigid and resistant to high temperatures. Thermoplastic composites, on the other hand, can be remelted and reshaped, offering greater flexibility in manufacturing processes. Both types of composites offer unique properties and cater to different applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
