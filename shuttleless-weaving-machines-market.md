<p><p>Shuttleless weaving machines have revolutionized the textile industry with their advanced technology. The main types are air jet weaving machines, water jet weaving machines, and rapier and projectile weaving machines. Air jet machines use air to propel the weft yarn through the shed, water jet machines use water for the same purpose, while rapier and projectile machines use a rapier or a projectile to carry the weft yarn across the shed. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different types of fabrics and production requirements.</p></p>
