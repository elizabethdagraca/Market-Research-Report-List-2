<p><ul><li>Disc-type Coupling</li><li>Synchronous Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic drive couplings are commonly classified into two main types: disc-type couplings and synchronous couplings. Disc-type couplings use magnetic discs to transfer torque from one shaft to another, allowing for flexibility in misalignment and vibration dampening. On the other hand, synchronous couplings use magnets to transmit torque without any physical contact, resulting in a more efficient and maintenance-free operation. Both types have their unique advantages and applications in various industries where reliable and efficient torque transmission is essential.</p></p>
