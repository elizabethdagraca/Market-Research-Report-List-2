<p><p>Camera Module Adhesives Market includes UV Curable Type and Non-UV Curable Type adhesives. UV Curable Type adhesives are cured using ultraviolet light, providing fast curing times and high bond strength. Non-UV Curable Type adhesives require a different curing method such as heat or moisture for bonding. Both types offer different advantages depending on the application requirements, with UV Curable Type adhesives being preferred for their speed and efficiency, while Non-UV Curable Type adhesives offer versatility in bonding materials.</p></p>
