<p><p>Embolotherapy market types include embolic agents, microspheres, embolic coils, liquid embolic agents, embolic plug systems, detachable balloons, support devices, microcatheters, and guidewires. Embolic agents are materials used to block blood flow in blood vessels, while microspheres are tiny beads used to selectively block vessels. Embolic coils are used to block blood flow and promote clotting, while liquid embolic agents are injected to block vessels. Embolic plug systems, detachable balloons, support devices, microcatheters, and guidewires are all tools used in embolotherapy procedures.</p></p>
