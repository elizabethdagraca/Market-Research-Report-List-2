<p><p>Turnout maintenance involves the regular upkeep and repair of railroad turnouts, which are critical components for controlling the direction of trains at rail junctions. This process includes cleaning, lubricating, adjusting, and replacing components such as switches and frogs to ensure smooth and safe operation of trains. Turnout maintenance is essential to prevent derailments, reduce operational disruptions, and extend the lifespan of railway infrastructure.</p><p>The Turnout Maintenance Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing investments in railway infrastructure projects worldwide. Additionally, technological advancements in predictive maintenance solutions, such as remote monitoring and condition-based maintenance, are contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Moreover, the adoption of automation and digitization in turnout maintenance processes is a key trend in the market.
