<p><p>Centralized Photovoltaic System market includes two main types - Low Concentrated Photovoltaic (LCPV) and High Concentrated Photovoltaic (HCPV) systems. LCPV systems use panels with minimal concentration ratios, allowing for cost-effective installations in large-scale projects. On the other hand, HCPV systems utilize higher concentration ratios, achieving higher energy output in smaller installations. Both systems are popular in centralized photovoltaic applications, offering different levels of efficiency and cost-effectiveness to meet varying project requirements.</p></p>
