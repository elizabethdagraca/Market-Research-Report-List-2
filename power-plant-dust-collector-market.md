<p><p>Power plant dust collectors are essential in maintaining air quality by capturing and removing dust particles from industrial emissions. The three main types in the market are Bag Dust Collectors, which use fabric bags to filter dust; Electrostatic Precipitators (ESP), which use electric charges to attract and remove dust particles; and Electrostatic-Bag Precipitators (EBP), which combine both technologies for enhanced dust removal efficiency. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of effectiveness, cost, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
