<p><p>Waterproof textiles market includes various types such as fluoropolymers, polyurethane (PU), polyester microfilament yarns, and other materials. Fluoropolymers are known for their excellent water repellent properties and are commonly used in outdoor gear and footwear. Polyurethane coatings provide durable waterproofing for fabrics and are often found in rain jackets and tents. Polyester microfilament yarns are lightweight, breathable, and offer water resistance, making them ideal for activewear and sportswear. Other materials in the market may include rubberized fabrics, laminates, and coatings that offer varying degrees of waterproofing for different applications.</p></p>
