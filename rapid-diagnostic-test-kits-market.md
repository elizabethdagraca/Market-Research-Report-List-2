<p><p>Rapid diagnostic test kits are used for quick and convenient testing in various healthcare settings. Different types of rapid diagnostic test kits include lateral flow tests, agglutination assays, immunochromatographic assays, and immunospot assays. Lateral flow tests use capillary action to detect the presence of certain analytes on a test strip. Agglutination assays utilize the clumping of particles to indicate a positive result. Immunochromatographic assays use antibodies to detect specific antigens, while immunospot assays quantify the number of cells producing a specific protein.</p></p>
