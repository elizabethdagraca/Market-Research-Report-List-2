<p><p>FBAR devices market includes various types such as Duplexers, Multiplexers, Filters, and Others. Duplexers are used to separate transmit and receive signals in communication systems. Multiplexers combine multiple signals into a single channel for transmission. Filters are used to limit specific frequencies in a signal. Other FBAR devices may include resonators, switches, and oscillators. These devices find applications in telecommunications, automotive, and aerospace industries for signal processing, routing, and frequency control.</p></p>
