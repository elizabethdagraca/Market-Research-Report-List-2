<p><p>Acute care ventilators are devices that assist patients with breathing in emergency medical situations. The market for these ventilators can be categorized into three main types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic. Electronic ventilators use electronic controls to deliver the appropriate levels of oxygen and air to the patient. Pneumatic ventilators operate using compressed gas to assist with breathing. Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine electronic controls with pneumatic mechanisms to provide respiratory support. Each type offers unique benefits and features for patients in need of acute care ventilation.</p></p>
