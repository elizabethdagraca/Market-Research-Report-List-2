<p><p>Carbon steel tubes and pipes market is categorized into two main types: welded tubes and pipes, and seamless tubes and pipes. Welded tubes and pipes are manufactured by welding the edges of steel strips together to form a tube, while seamless tubes and pipes are produced by extruding a solid steel billet through a die to form a tube without any seams. Both types are widely used in various industries for conveying fluids, gas, and structural applications.</p></p>
