<p><p>Induction heating generators are classified into two types based on the frequency they operate at: Medium Frequency Generators and High Frequency Generators. Medium frequency generators typically operate in the range of 500 Hz to 10 kHz, offering a balance between efficiency and cost. On the other hand, High Frequency Generators operate at frequencies above 10 kHz, providing faster heating rates and more precise control. Both types of generators are widely used in industrial processes such as heating, brazing, and soldering.</p></p>
