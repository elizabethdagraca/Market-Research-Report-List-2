<p><p>The Residential Smoke Alarm (Smoke Detector) market can be categorized into three main types: Photoelectric Smoke Alarms, Ionization Smoke Alarms, and Combination Smoke Alarms. </p><p>Photoelectric Smoke Alarms use light sensors to detect smoke particles, making them suitable for smoldering fires. Ionization Smoke Alarms, on the other hand, use radioactive material to ionize the air and detect fast-flaming fires. Combination Smoke Alarms combine both technologies for comprehensive fire detection. Each type has its own advantages and is designed to provide early warning and protection in residential settings.</p></p>
