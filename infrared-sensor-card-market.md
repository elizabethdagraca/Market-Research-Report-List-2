<p><p>Infrared sensor cards are available in various types such as IR, NIR, UV, and VIS markets. IR (infrared) sensors detect infrared light, mainly used for night vision and temperature sensing. NIR (near-infrared) sensors detect light with wavelengths slightly longer than visible light, suitable for applications like food quality control. UV (ultraviolet) sensors detect ultraviolet light for applications like counterfeit detection. VIS (visible) sensors detect visible light, commonly used in proximity sensors and color sensors. Each type of sensor card caters to specific market needs and applications.</p></p>
