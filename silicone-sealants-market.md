<p><p>Silicone sealants are used in various industries and applications, with different types catering to specific needs. Pressure sensitive sealants adhere instantly upon application of pressure, while radiation curing sealants cure quickly when exposed to UV light. Room temperature vulcanizing sealants harden at ambient temperatures, offering convenience in application. Thermoset sealants require heat to cure, providing durable and long-lasting seals. Each type serves a unique purpose in the market, offering versatility and effectiveness in different environments and applications.</p></p>
