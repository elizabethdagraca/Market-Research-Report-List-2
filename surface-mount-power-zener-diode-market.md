<p><p>Surface mount power zener diodes are available in various types including single, dual, and triple/triple opposing configurations. Single zener diodes have one zener diode in the package, suitable for basic voltage regulation applications. Dual zener diodes feature two zener diodes in the same package, ideal for applications requiring dual voltage regulation. Triple/triple opposing zener diodes have three zener diodes arranged in opposing pairs, offering precision voltage regulation and increased reliability. Each type caters to specific requirements in electronic circuits, providing flexibility in design and implementation.</p></p>
