<p><p>Rugs and carpets market types include woven, needle felt, tufted, and others. Woven rugs and carpets are made by interlacing threads on a loom. Needle felt carpets are created by punching fibers through a base material. Tufted rugs are made by pushing loops of yarn through a backing material. Other types may include knotted, flat weave, and hooked rugs. Each type of rug or carpet offers different textures, styles, and levels of durability to meet various consumer preferences.</p></p>
