<p><ul><li>Square Mesh</li><li>Rectangular Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Floor heating mesh is commonly available in two main types: square mesh and rectangular mesh. Square mesh is typically characterized by equal spacing between horizontal and vertical wires, creating a uniform grid pattern. This type of mesh is often preferred for its simplicity and ease of installation. On the other hand, rectangular mesh features varying spacing between horizontal and vertical wires, allowing for more flexibility in customizing the layout to fit specific floor dimensions. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
