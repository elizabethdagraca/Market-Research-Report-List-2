<p><p>High-purity Aluminum Nitride (AlN) Powder and Granules Market includes three main types: Direct Nitridation Method, Carbothermal Reduction, and Nitridation Method. </p><p>Direct Nitridation Method involves reacting aluminum with nitrogen gas, producing high-purity AlN powder. Carbothermal Reduction process involves reacting aluminum oxide with carbon and nitrogen gas to form AlN powder. Nitridation method involves reacting aluminum powder with nitrogen gas at high temperatures to obtain AlN powder. These methods are commonly used in the production of high-purity Aluminum Nitride products.</p></p>
