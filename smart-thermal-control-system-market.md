<p><ul><li>Passive Smart Thermal Control System</li><li>Active Smart Thermal Control System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive Smart Thermal Control System refers to systems that passively regulate temperature through materials that can absorb, store, and release heat. This type of system does not require external power sources. </p><p>On the other hand, Active Smart Thermal Control System involves systems that actively use external power sources to regulate and control temperature through mechanisms like fans, pumps, and sensors. These systems are more advanced and provide greater control over temperature regulation in various environments.</p></p>
