<p><p>The Electro Optical System market includes various types such as Infrared Type and Laser Type systems. On the other hand, Laser Type systems emit laser beams to measure distances, aim at targets, and provide guidance for various applications such as range finding and target acquisition. Both types play critical roles in defense, surveillance, and navigation industries.</p></p>
