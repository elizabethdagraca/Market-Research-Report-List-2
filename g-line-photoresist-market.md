<p><p>G-line photoresist market types include photopolymerization, light decomposition type, and photocrosslinked. Photopolymerization photoresists use light to trigger a chemical reaction that changes the material's properties. Light decomposition type photoresists undergo a decomposition process when exposed to light. Photocrosslinked photoresists form a network of crosslinked polymers when exposed to light. These different types of photoresists cater to various applications and allow for precise and controlled patterning in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing and microelectronics.</p></p>
