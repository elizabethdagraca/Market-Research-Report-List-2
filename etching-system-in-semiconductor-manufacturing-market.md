<p><p>The etching system in semiconductor manufacturing refers to the process of removing material from a semiconductor wafer to create patterns or structures. There are two main types of etching systems: dry etch systems and wet etch systems. Dry etch systems use a plasma or vapor phase to etch the material, while wet etch systems use liquid chemicals. Both systems are essential in semiconductor manufacturing for creating intricate patterns and structures on the wafer to build electronic devices.</p></p>
