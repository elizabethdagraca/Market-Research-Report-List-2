<p><strong>The Clamp Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Current transformer (AC) clamp meters</li><li>Hall effect (AC/DC) clamp meters</li><li>Flexible (AC) clamp meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clamp meters come in different types to cater to various needs in the market. Current transformer (AC) clamp meters are used to measure alternating current, while Hall effect (AC/DC) clamp meters can measure both alternating and direct current. Flexible (AC) clamp meters, on the other hand, have a bendable head for accessing tight spaces and awkward angles. Each type of clamp meter offers unique features and benefits, making it suitable for specific applications and industries.</p></p>
