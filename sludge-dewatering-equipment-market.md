<p><p>Sludge dewatering equipment market types include screw press, rotator disc press, centrifuges, belt filter press, and other. Screw press technology involves using a rotating screw to dewater sludge. Rotator disc press uses a series of rotating discs to separate water from sludge. Centrifuges use centrifugal force to separate solid and liquid components. Belt filter press utilizes a series of belts to filter and dewater sludge. Other types of dewatering equipment may include vacuum filters, filter presses, and gravity thickeners. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, cost, and effectiveness in sludge dewatering processes.</p></p>
