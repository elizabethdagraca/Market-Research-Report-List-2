<p><p>Rayon carbon fiber can be categorized into three main types: continuous, long, and short. Continuous carbon fiber is made up of continuous filaments of carbon, providing high strength and stiffness. Long carbon fibers are shorter than continuous fibers but still offer good strength and stiffness properties. Short carbon fibers are the smallest in length and are typically used to reinforce composite materials. Each type of rayon carbon fiber caters to different applications and requirements in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and sporting goods.</p></p>
