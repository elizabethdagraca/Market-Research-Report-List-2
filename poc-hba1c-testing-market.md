Boronate affinity method involves the interaction of HbA1C with a boronate compound. Other methods in the market include ion-exchange chromatography, enzymatic assays, and capillary electrophoresis. These various testing types offer different advantages in terms of accuracy, speed, and convenience for point-of-care HbA1C testing.</p></p>
